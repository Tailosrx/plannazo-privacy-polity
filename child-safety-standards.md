# Child Safety Standards – Plannazo

**Last updated: April 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Plannazo is a social planning application that allows users to create, share, and join events and plans. We are fully committed to the safety of children and minors on our platform. This document describes our standards against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and outlines the measures we take to detect, prevent, and respond to any form of child exploitation or abuse.

---

## 2. Zero Tolerance Policy

Plannazo has a strict **zero-tolerance policy** toward any content or behavior that involves the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors. This includes, but is not limited to:

- Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) in any form
- - Grooming or solicitation of minors
  - - Trafficking or exploitation of minors
    - - Any content that sexualizes individuals under the age of 18
     
      - Any user found to be engaging in such behavior will be **immediately and permanently banned** from the platform, and the relevant content will be removed without notice.
     
      - ---

      ## 3. Reporting Mechanisms

      Plannazo provides clear and accessible in-app mechanisms for users to report child safety concerns:

      - **In-app reporting**: Every user profile and event can be reported directly through the app using the "Report" feature.
      - - **Email reporting**: Users can report concerns to our dedicated safety contact at **kevin@plannazo.es**.
        - - **Automated detection**: We implement automated tools to detect and flag potentially harmful content involving minors.
         
          - All reports are reviewed promptly by our safety team.
         
          - ---

          ## 4. Response to Reports

          Upon receiving a report involving potential child exploitation or abuse:

          1. The reported content is reviewed within **24 hours**.
          2. 2. If confirmed, the content is **immediately removed** and the offending account is **suspended or banned**.
             3. 3. We **report confirmed cases of CSAM to the appropriate authorities**, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) via CyberTipline, as required by applicable law.
                4. 4. We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations.
                  
                   5. ---
                  
                   6. ## 5. Compliance with Laws
                  
                   7. Plannazo complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including:
                  
                   8. - The **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**
                      - - **EU regulations** on child protection online
                        - - Local laws in all jurisdictions where the app is available
                         
                          - ---

                          ## 6. Age Restrictions

                          Plannazo is intended for users **aged 16 and over**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that a user is under the minimum age, their account will be **immediately suspended** and their data will be **deleted**.

                          ---

                          ## 7. Content Moderation

                          We employ a combination of automated and human moderation to ensure our platform remains safe:

                          - **Automated filtering** of potentially harmful content
                          - - **Human review** of flagged content
                            - - **Proactive monitoring** of public events and profiles
